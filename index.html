📋 Smart Report Import

Paste your full vet visit report. The system auto-updates each goat's records, flags health issues and generates drug orders.

Report format per animal (one per line):
TAG | STATUS | WEIGHT | CONDITION | TREATMENT | DRUGS NEEDED | NOTES
Example: CIK002 | Alive | 18kg | Good | Dewormed | Albendazole 10ml | Follow up in 3 weeks
Drug names are auto-matched to Vet Shop catalog and create orders.
